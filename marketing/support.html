Common questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

On the phone app: open your Google Play account → Subscriptions → Education Academy → Cancel. On the website: open the app, go to Settings → Manage subscription, which opens the secure billing portal.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds follow the policy of where you bought it — Google Play (app) or Stripe (website) — and your local consumer rights still apply. Contact us and we’ll help point you to the right place.

How do I delete my account and data?

Open the app → Grown‑ups area → account settings → Delete my account & data. This removes your account and associated learner records.

The AI tutor isn’t responding

AI features need an internet connection. If you’re offline, the app falls back to saved practice questions. If you’re online and it still fails, try again shortly.

Are the advanced courses accredited?

No — they’re revision and exam‑preparation aids aligned to the subject areas, not accredited qualifications. See our Terms for details.
