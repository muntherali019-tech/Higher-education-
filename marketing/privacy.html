Education Academy Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE]. This policy explains how [YOUR COMPANY/NAME] ("we") handles information in the Education Academy app and website.

Education Academy is intended for use by children with the involvement of a parent, guardian or teacher. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with COPPA (US), the UK Age-Appropriate Design Code ("Children's Code"), and UK/EU GDPR.

Information we collect

  • Account information (for parents/teachers who create an account): email address and a display name.
  • Child profile information: a display name and key stage, entered by a parent or teacher.
  • Learning progress: questions answered, accuracy, stars, streaks, goals and tasks.
  • Photos for homework marking / scan & solve: when a user chooses to take or upload a photo, the image is sent to our AI provider to generate feedback. Images are processed transiently to produce a response and are not stored by us afterwards.

We do not use third-party advertising, we do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs in the child experience, and we do not sell personal information.

The mascot's voice uses your device's built-in text-to-speech by default — the words are generated on your device and are not sent to us. If you enable the optional premium voice, the short text Mochi reads is sent to our voice provider to generate the audio. Speaking practice (in Languages) uses your device or browser's speech recognition to check pronunciation; that audio is handled by your device/browser's service and is not stored by us.

How we use information

  • To run the learning features (questions, marking, explanations).
  • To save and sync progress across a child's devices when an account is used.
  • To let parents and teachers set and track goals and tasks.

Service providers

We use a small number of processors strictly to provide the service:

  • Anthropic — provides the AI used for questions, homework marking and explanations. Photos and question text are sent to this service to generate a response.
  • [YOUR HOSTING / DATABASE PROVIDER] — stores account and progress data securely.
  • ElevenLabs (optional) — if you enable the premium voice, short text is sent here to generate the spoken audio.

These providers are bound to protect the data and use it only to provide their service to us.

Children's privacy & parental consent

Accounts are created by adults (parents or teachers). Purchases and links out of the app sit behind a parental gate. Where required by law, a parent or guardian must consent before a child uses features that send data (such as photo marking). A parent or teacher can review, correct, or delete a child's information at any time.

Data retention and deletion

We keep account and progress data only as long as the account is active. You can delete data at any time:

  • In the app: open the Parent & Teacher portal → Delete my account & data, or remove an individual child from their profile.
  • By request: email [PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL] and we will delete your account and associated data.

A web form for deletion requests is available at [YOUR DELETION REQUEST URL].

Your rights
